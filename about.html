What is The Green Notebook?

The Green Note Book is a 100% non-profit organization focused on planting trees through our notetaking application.

What is Our Goal?

The purpose of The Green Notebook is to raise awareness and funds for the climate action and provide funds to continue the preservation of the Earth as much as possible.

How Do We Make Money?

All the money made from ad revenue goes to these charities:

  • One Tree Planted
  • Trees for the Future
  • Trees for Cities
  • International Tree Foundation

Is the Green Notebook Trustworthy?

We try our best to be as transparent and accountable as possible. The Green Notebook will take any test to prove accountability from a trusted source.
